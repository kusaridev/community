# Kusari Community

Welcome to the space for Kusari's free tier offerings.
To submit feature requests or bug reports, please see the [Discussions tab](https://github.com/kusaridev/community/discussions).
If you need to share information privately, you can email [opensource@kusari.dev](mailto:opensource@kusari.dev).
For paid plan support, contact [support@kusari.dev](mailto:support@kusari.dev).
Support is offered on a best-effort basis.

Participation this community is subject to the [code of conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.MD).
We reserve the right to moderate content and limit participation.
